---

This guide walks you through creating your first product, connecting PostHog, and querying your user intelligence.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [app.oriyn.ai](https://app.oriyn.ai) and create your account. After signing in, you'll land on the dashboard where you can create your first product.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a product">
    Select **New product** from the dashboard. Give your product a name, an optional description, and any relevant URLs — your marketing site, app, or documentation. Oriyn uses this information to build an initial structured understanding of what your product does and who it serves.

    The more context you provide upfront, the more accurate the initial synthesis will be. You can update these fields at any time.

    <Note>
      After you save your product, Oriyn automatically runs context synthesis in the background. This produces a structured summary covering your product's core features, target users, and value proposition. It typically completes within a minute.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect PostHog">
    Navigate to **Integrations** in your product settings and select **PostHog**. Oriyn uses OAuth to connect to your PostHog account — click **Connect** and follow the authorization flow. No API keys to manage manually.

    Once connected, Oriyn has access to your PostHog event data and can use it to power enrichment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run enrichment and explore your intelligence">
    With PostHog connected, trigger enrichment from your product's **Enrichment** tab. Oriyn reads your behavioral event data and generates:

    - **Personas** — AI-generated user archetypes with behavioral traits and estimated share of your user base
    - **Patterns** — Behavioral sequences derived from real event flows
    - **Direction** — Prioritized recommendations grounded in what your data signals

    Enrichment typically takes a few minutes depending on data volume. When `enrichment_status` reaches `ready`, your intelligence is available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Query your users">
    You can ask Oriyn questions in plain language — from the dashboard, the API, or the CLI.

    **Install the CLI:**

    ```bash
    curl -fsSL https://install.oriyn.ai | bash
    ```

    **Authenticate:**

    ```bash
    oriyn login
    ```

    **Run your first query:**

    ```bash
    oriyn query "Who are my power users?"
    ```

    You can also call the query endpoint directly from your own code or pipelines:

    <CodeGroup>

    ```bash cURL
    curl -X POST https://api.oriyn.ai/v1/query \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer <your-token>" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d '{"prompt": "Who are my power users?"}'
    ```

    ```json Response
    {
      "answer": "Your power users are primarily in the 'Builder' persona — engineers and technical leads who log in daily, use the API directly, and have created more than 5 products. They represent approximately 18% of your active user base."
    }
    ```

    </CodeGroup>

    To get your Bearer token for direct API calls, log in at [app.oriyn.ai](https://app.oriyn.ai) or run `oriyn login` with the CLI. See [Authentication](/api-reference/authentication) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
