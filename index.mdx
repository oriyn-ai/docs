## How Oriyn works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a product">
    Add your product by name and description. Oriyn researches it and builds a structured context automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your analytics">
    Link your PostHog account. Oriyn pulls real behavioral event data to ground its intelligence in actual usage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run enrichment">
    Trigger enrichment to generate personas, behavioral patterns, and prescriptive direction — all derived from your users' actual behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Query and experiment">
    Ask natural-language questions about your users, or run hypothesis experiments to get a verdict before shipping.
  </Step>
</Steps>
